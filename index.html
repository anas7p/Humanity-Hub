The Issues

Five topics. One shared humanity.

Each section explores a challenge facing our world — with facts, stories and simple ways to help.

01 — Poverty

When survival is a daily struggle

Over 700 million people live in extreme poverty. Learn the causes, effects and ways to help.

 02 — War & Conflict

The cost is measured in lives

Conflict drives the largest refugee crisis since WWII. Explore its impact and the path to peace.

 03 — Climate Change

Our only home is heating up

CO₂ is at a 3-million-year high. Try the carbon footprint tool and discover solutions.

 04 — Cyberbullying

Words on screens leave real wounds

1 in 3 young people has experienced cyberbullying. Take the recognition quiz.

 05 — Mental Health

Your mind deserves care too

Nearly 1 billion people live with a mental illness. Breathe, reflect and find support.
