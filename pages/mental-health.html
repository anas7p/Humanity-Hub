05 — Mental Health

Your mind deserves care too

Anxiety, depression and burnout affect nearly 1 billion people. It's okay not to be okay — and it's okay to ask for help.

Anxiety, depression & student stress

Pressure at school, uncertainty about the future and constant connectivity can overwhelm young minds. Recognising the signs is the first step to healing.

Why support matters

No one should face these struggles alone. Friends, family, teachers and professionals can make recovery possible.
