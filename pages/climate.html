03 — Climate Change

Our only home is heating up

CO₂ levels are at a 3-million-year high. The decisions of this decade will shape centuries.

What's happening

Global warming, pollution, deforestation, ocean acidification, species extinction and extreme weather are accelerating.

Natural disasters

Rising temperatures fuel stronger storms, longer droughts and devastating floods that displace communities worldwide.
